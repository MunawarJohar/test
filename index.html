Computer Vision

In the "Melanoma Detection Project," we applied advanced image analysis and machine learning techniques to develop a model that assists in the early detection of melanoma from dermatological images. Our team worked to preprocess and augment image data, implement convolutional neural networks for feature extraction, and train classification algorithms capable of distinguishing between benign and malignant skin lesions. We aimed to provide a diagnostic tool that enhances the accuracy and speed of melanoma screening for healthcare professionals, potentially improving patient outcomes through early intervention. This project exemplifies the integration of AI in healthcare, demonstrating how machine learning can be a pivotal resource in medical diagnostics and the ongoing pursuit of proactive, data-driven patient care..
